
A **Content Security Policy (CSP)** is one of the most effective ways to **prevent attacks like Cross-Site Scripting (XSS)** and **code injection** on your website.

==**CSP (Content Security Policy)** is a **security header** that tells browsers **what content is allowed to load and execute** on your website==. 

It helps prevent:
- **XSS attacks** (e.g., malicious scripts injected into your page)
- **Clickjacking**
- **Data injection or leaks**
