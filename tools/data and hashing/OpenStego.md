
`OpenStego` lets you **hide information inside images** or **watermark them invisibly**.

**Purpose**:
1. **Data Hiding** – hides secret files/messages inside image files without visibly altering them.
2. **Digital Watermarking** – embeds invisible watermarks in images for copyright/authentication.

- **Key Features**:
    - Password protection & encryption of hidden data.
    - Simple GUI and command-line interface.
    - Cross-platform (works on Windows, Linux, macOS).
    - Lightweight and easy to use.

- **Use Cases**:
    - Sending confidential data securely.
    - Protecting ownership of digital images.
